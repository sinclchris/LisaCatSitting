How I work

Experienced and reliable cat & small‑pet care and dog walking. Daytime visits, pet sitting, elderly dog visits, group and solo walks — tailored to you and your pets.

* At Lisa's Cat Sitting your pet's safety and security is my top priority. I am fully insured and DBS checked for your peace of mind. * With years of hands-on experience in pet care, I bring a professional approach to every job, ensuring your pets are well cared for and comfortable. * I understand that every pet has its own needs and preferences. My services are tailored to fit the individual care requirements of each pet. * I pride myself on being prompt, dependable, and trustworthy, so you can count on Lisa's Cat Sitting to keep your pets' routine on track.

  • Food & fresh water replenished
  • Feeding area cleaned; timer bowls set if needed
  • Administer medication as directed
  • Litter tray cleaned and refreshed
  • Daily photo reports
  • Vet visit arranged if required

Prices from £10 per 30‑min cat/small‑pet visit (Dereham). Solo dog walks from £14 per hour (Dereham).
