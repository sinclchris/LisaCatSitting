How we work

Lisa

Experienced and reliable cat, small pet care service and dog walking. Covering Dereham and the surrounding area. Offering daytime cat and small pet visits, pet sitting, elderly dog visits, group, solo dog walks and more!

To fit you and your pets needs. Fully insured, DBS checked and first aid trained. Prices for cat and small pet care start from £10 per 30 minute visit (Dereham) Prices for solo dog walks start from £14 per 1 hour walk (Dereham) Here are a few things that I cover on my visits:

  • Food and water replenishment.
  • Cleaning of feeding area and setting up timer bowls if required.
  • Fuss - on your cats own terms!
  • Administer medication.
  • If your cat becomes unwell while you’re away, with your permission I will make arrangements for them to see a vet. If I cannot get hold of you or your emergency contact then I will make my own judgement.
  • Cleaning of litter tray and area.
  • My daily reports will include photos.

I offer an initial pre-meeting for all new clients that book in advance. I will pop round about a week before your dates to see where everything is and to collect a key. If your cat does not have free access to a cat flap I would recommend that they are kept indoors with a litter tray for the duration of the sit.

If you are looking for a dog walker, puppy or elderly dog visit whilst you are away or at work then look no further. I’m confident and experienced in exercising dogs in a safe way. I have different dog walking times available Monday-Friday. Once home I will make sure your dog has a full bowl of fresh water before leaving them. I can also feed your dog if requested before I leave them.
