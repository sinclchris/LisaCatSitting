Lisas Cat Sitting

Daily home cat sitting service, caring for your cats while you are away. Covering Dereham and surrounding area. Whether you need help as a one off, regular occasions or you are going off on holiday we all want to be assured as cat parents that when we go away our cats are going to be well looked after. My services therefore include, feeding and laying fresh water, cleaning food and water bowls, cleaning litter boxes, giving medication and spending time interacting, playing and cuddling your cats when you are away from home. I offer one or two daily 30 minute visits depending on your requirements.
Cats can get stressed when taken to a new environment, so by staying in their own space they are much more relaxed. If your cats are like my petals they like to be in their own environment rather than being placed in a cattery so please, consider my services.
