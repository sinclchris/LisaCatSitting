About us

Whether you need a one‑off, regular visits, or you’re going on holiday, we’ll keep your cats comfortable at home and your routine intact.

Visits are typically 30 minutes once or twice a day based on your needs. Many cats are far less stressed staying in their own space than going to a cattery.
