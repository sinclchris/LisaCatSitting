Meet Lisa

I'm Lisa — a Norfolk-based pet sitter and dog walker based in Dereham. I started Lisa's Pet Sitting because I believe pets are happiest in their own home, surrounded by familiar smells and routines, rather than in an unfamiliar cattery or kennel.

Every visit is tailored to your pet's individual needs. Whether that's a solo walk for an elderly dog, twice-daily cat visits while you're on holiday, or medication administered on a strict schedule — I'll keep things as normal as possible for them.

I've also cared for a range of other animals including rabbits, tortoises, chickens and fish. If you have an exotic or more unusual pet, get in touch and I'll let you know if I can help.
